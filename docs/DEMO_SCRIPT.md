### Voiceover Tips
- Start with energy — hook them in the first 10 seconds
- Slow down during technical explanations
- Emphasize key numbers: "48 hours," "80% confidence," "30% allocation"
- Pause after big reveals ("...the system publishes the story AND executes the trade")
- End with confidence, not hype

### Music
- Upbeat electronic/tech background (low volume)
- Build intensity during the trade execution scene
- Calm during architecture explanation
- Strong finish for the close

### B-Roll Suggestions
- Stock footage: trading floors, data centers, code scrolling
- Animated transitions between scenes
- Particle effects on the narrative bubble map
- Glowing highlight on key UI elements during voiceover
